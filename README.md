## ğŸ“– How to Use

1. **Launch the app** - You'll see the main boom-box window in the top-right corner (it's shy, so it hides there)

2. **Select your devices** - Choose your microphone and camera from the dropdowns (they auto-select defaults, because we're helpful like that)

3. **Position your camera** - The circular camera window appears in the bottom-left. Drag it wherever you want (it's your world, we're just living in it)

4. **Toggle blur** - Click the "Background Blur" checkbox if you want to hide your laundry pile

5. **Start recording** - Click "Record" and watch the magic happen (or don't, since the main window won't be in the recording anyway)

6. **Pause/Resume** - Use the "Pause" button if you need to take a break (we all need breaks)

7. **Stop recording** - Click "Record" again to stop. Your masterpiece will be saved to `Downloads/boom-box`
